FAQs

Q: How do I log out?

Proceed to the main home page for the IMS home page and push the green, log out button in the lower right hand corner of the screen. Wait for acknowledgement that the log out has been completed.

Q: What do the analytics show?

The analytics show a comparison between current sale trends that can be toggled against week on week, month on month and year on year sales. They are designed to help you keep certain items in stock during busy seasonal promotional periods.

Q: Why do I not have access to admin?

Only the warehouse manager has access to admin. You must be logged in as the warehouse manager in order to undertake admin functions.
